## Recommended Publishing Strategy

1. **Start with Priority 1 & 2** — Security and Frontend topics build your core authority and match your resume positioning.
2. **Publish 2 articles per week** — one technical deep-dive (Security/Frontend/Blockchain) + one growth/career topic.
3. **Cluster your content** — group 5–8 related articles together with strong internal links to build topic authority faster.
4. **Target long-tail keywords early** — "How to set up Trivy in GitHub Actions", "SvelteKit Supabase Row-Level Security" — these rank faster than broad terms.
5. **Repurpose every article** — turn each blog post into a LinkedIn post, a Twitter/X thread, and a short YouTube video or Reel.
